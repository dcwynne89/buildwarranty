Privacy Policy

Effective: June 7, 2026

What We Collect

When you register for an API key, we store:

  • Your email address (for account identification)
  • A hashed version of your API key (we never store the raw key)
  • Monthly usage counts

What We Don't Collect

We do not store:

  • Warranty certificate content or client data
  • PDF files (generated on the fly and immediately returned)
  • Business names, addresses, or coverage details from your warranties
  • Project descriptions, exclusions, or terms entered in your forms

Data Processing

Warranty data is processed in memory only. Once your PDF is generated and returned in the API response, all warranty data is immediately discarded. No warranty content is ever written to disk, logged, or stored in a database.

Third-Party Services

  • Netlify — hosting and serverless functions
  • Google AdSense — advertising on our consumer site (uses cookies)
  • Google Firebase — optional account authentication and data storage

Cookies

Our site uses cookies from Google AdSense for ad personalization. You can opt out at adssettings.google.com.

Optional Account & Data Storage

All Build tools work fully without an account. If you choose to sign in, we store:

  • Your email address and display name
  • Documents you explicitly save (e.g. warranty certificates)
  • Client contact information you enter

This data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore and associated with your account. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by emailing buildpdf.help@gmail.com.

If you do not create an account, no document data is stored.

Data Deletion

To delete your API key and associated data, email buildpdf.help@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email buildpdf.help@gmail.com.
