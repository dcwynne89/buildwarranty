About BuildWarranty

I built BuildWarranty because too many contractors and service providers make warranty promises verbally — and then have nothing to show for it when a client calls back a year later. "You said it was guaranteed" turns into a he-said-she-said situation that erodes trust and costs money.

The solution is simple: put it in writing. But most people don't because creating a warranty document feels like it requires a lawyer or expensive software. It doesn't. You need a form and a PDF.

What I Actually Built

A form on the left, a live warranty certificate preview on the right. Enter your business details, the client info, warranty type, duration, coverage, exclusions, and claim process — then download a clean PDF with dual signature lines. The expiration date auto-calculates from your start date and duration. Done in under two minutes.

The preview updates as you type, so you can see exactly what your client will receive before downloading. The PDF is generated server-side so it looks identical in every PDF reader on every device.

Who It's For

Roofers, HVAC technicians, electricians, plumbers, appliance installers, general contractors, manufacturers, auto mechanics — anyone who stands behind their work and wants to prove it. If you finish a job and your client asks "do I get a warranty?", this tool exists so your answer is always "already in your inbox."

Privacy

Your warranty data isn't stored anywhere. The PDF is generated, returned in the HTTP response, and that's it. Nothing is written to a database. Nothing is logged beyond basic request metrics. Your clients' details stay between you and them.

Part of the Build Ecosystem

BuildWarranty is part of a growing set of tools for independent workers: BuildInvoice, BuildReceipt, BuildQuotes, BuildChangeOrder, BuildPDF, and BuildCertificates. Same philosophy across all of them: tools that do one thing well, no login required.
